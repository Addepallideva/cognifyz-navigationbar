Explore the fascinating world of color theory and its impact on human emotions.

Blog

Colors can evoke powerful emotions and convey different meanings in various cultures and contexts. Here's a breakdown of colors and their associations with human feelings:

Primary Colors:

  • Red: Passion, energy, love, anger.
  • Blue: Calmness, trust, loyalty, sadness.
  • Yellow: Happiness, optimism, caution, anxiety.

Secondary Colors:

  • Green: Nature, growth, harmony, balance.
  • Orange: Creativity, enthusiasm, playfulness, frustration.
  • Purple: Luxury, creativity, wisdom, spirituality.

Tertiary Colors:

  • Pink: Love, nurturing, softness, vulnerability.
  • Brown: Earthiness, stability, reliability, boredom.
  • Gray: Neutrality, balance, sophistication, depression.
  • Black: Power, elegance, death, mourning.
  • White: Purity, innocence, clarity, cleanliness.
